Andean Region

The Amazon is the Southern part of Colombia and it is mostly covered by rainforests/jungle. It is part of the huge Amazon rainforest.

This region is composed by the departments of Amazonas, Caquetá, Guainía, Guaviare, Putumayo and Vaupés.
